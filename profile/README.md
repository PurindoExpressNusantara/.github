## Who we are
Purindo Express is a logistics services company focused on safe, planned, and timely cargo delivery solutions. We help businesses and individuals manage their shipments with clear processes, open communication, and reliable service.

## What we do
Whatever the type and quantity of goods you need to ship, we are ready to assist with flexible and reliable services. From pickup and delivery at airport cargo terminals, door handling services for East Kalimantan, to port-to-port air freight throughout Indonesia—all can be tailored to your needs.

Don't hesitate to consult with us, as our team is ready to assist you 24/7, free of charge. We believe that good communication is key to smooth logistics, so please contact us anytime with questions or to discuss your shipment.
